# Cast — Onboarding

4 screens for the casting app.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Cast

**Headline:** Your Mac, on the big screen.

**Body:** Cast sends videos, music, photos, and more from your Mac to any Chromecast, Google TV, Android TV, or AirPlay device on your network.

**Illustration:** A flat illustration of a MacBook on the left, connected via wireless broadcast waves to a large TV on the right. The broadcast waves are in teal (primary brand color). Clean, dark background, cinematic feel.

**CTA Button:** "Find Devices →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right)

---

## Screen 2 — Discovering Devices

**Headline:** Looking for devices…

**Body:** Cast is scanning your network for Chromecast, Google TV, Android TV, and AirPlay devices. Make sure your TV and Mac are on the same Wi-Fi network.

**Illustration:** A scanning animation illustration: concentric circles radiating outward from a central TV icon (like radar). Small device icons (Chromecast puck, Apple TV box, smart TV) appearing at the edges of the radar as they are "found." Teal circles. Dark background.

**CTA Button:** (disabled, shows spinner) "Scanning…"

**Secondary text:** "Make sure your TV and Mac are on the same network"

---

## Screen 3 — Found Devices

**Headline:** [N] devices found.

**Body:** Select a device to start casting. The device will show a cast icon when you're connected.

**Illustration:** A grid of 2-3 device cards showing: Chromecast TV (with Google's colors), Android TV box, Apple TV. Each card has a device icon, name, and a small "tap to cast" indicator. The first card has a teal glow/border indicating it's the recommended/first device.

**CTA Button:** "Start Casting"

**Or:** "Cast Again" if a device was already set up

---

## Screen 4 — You're Casting

**Headline:** Connected.

**Body:** [Content name] is now playing on [Device Name]. A Cast icon will appear in your menu bar. Click it to stop or switch devices.

**Illustration:** A celebratory but minimal illustration: the selected TV now shows a "playing" state — a video frame or music note visible on the screen. A subtle teal glow around the TV. A Cast icon badge in the corner with a green checkmark. A small Cast menu bar icon in the bottom right. Dark background. Confident, not overdone.

**CTA Button:** "Stop Casting" (initially, then changes to "Done")
